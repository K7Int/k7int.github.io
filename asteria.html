Asteria

"This is not an app.
This is my personal computer for my life."

A local-first, retro desktop application that treats your life like a filesystem. Beige windows, a pixel desktop, draggable panels — everything is a memory file.

License: MIT — free, open, yours.
Storage: SQLite · WAL mode · portable
Privacy: Zero telemetry. No cloud. No accounts.

README.txt
In a world of clouds,
be a filesystem.
Your computer. Your data. Your life.

K7I Asteria turns your life into a filesystem. Books become files. Movies become files. Trips become folders. Photos become assets. Journals become documents. Workouts become logs. Places become nodes. Every memory is a file — linked, searchable, yours.

Tech Stack
DesktopTauri v2 (Rust + WebView)
BackendRust, SQLx, SQLite (WAL)
FrontendReact 18, TypeScript, Vite
UITailwind CSS, Framer Motion, Zustand
Window SystemCustom retro OS emulation
AIOllama / OpenAI / Anthropic / local
Architecture
