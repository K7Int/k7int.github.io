README.txt
Humans spend nearly half their lives at night,
yet almost every application focuses on the daytime.
Noctis exists to preserve the forgotten half.

K7I Noctis is a private archive — not a sleep tracker, not a wellness app. It is a quiet companion that preserves the beauty of the night. Every photograph, walk, book, conversation, piece of music, and quiet moment becomes part of an ever-growing nocturnal archive.

Features
  • Night Stories — Dreams, journal, music, books, walks, weather, photos, and more
  • Dream Gallery — Search by mood, filter lucid dreams, track intensity
  • Timeline — Year-by-year chronological view of your archive
  • Collections — Browse all content by type
  • Search — Full-text search across everything (Ctrl+K)
  • Home Dashboard — Moon phase, sunset/sunrise, recent nights
  • Statistics — Gentle, reflective insights
