About

Why K7I exists.

We believe software went wrong when it started requiring constant internet and sending everything to the cloud. We build tools that run locally, respect your hardware, and never phone home.

Privacy and local-first

No cloud accounts. Your encrypted data lives in SQLite files on your disk. Back up wherever you want. Own your digital footprint.

Open SQLite schemas

Every tool uses standard SQLite. You can query, inspect, and migrate your own data with any SQL tool. No proprietary formats.

Built by developers

Clean CLI interfaces, structured JSON output, Unix pipe support, and open schemas. Scriptable from day one.
