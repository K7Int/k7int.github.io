ReadMe.txt
Software should belong to its users — not the cloud, not subscriptions, not advertisers.
Your computer. Your data. Your rules.

We started K7I because we were tired of software that disappears when a server shuts down, or changes when a company pivots. Every tool we build stores your data locally, works offline by default, and stays with you as long as you want it. No accounts. No hidden fees. Just honest software that respects your time and your machine.

System Information

Everything we build follows three core beliefs. These aren't just words — they shape every line of code we write.

Spec 01
Privacy-first
Your data stays on your machine. No accounts, no tracking, no cloud dependency. What's yours stays yours.
Spec 02
Open & portable
Plain SQLite files and open schemas. Query, back up, and move your data anywhere. No vendor lock-in, ever.
Spec 03
Made by hand
Clean interfaces, small footprint, and a sense of craft. Every detail is intentional. Built to be useful, not flashy.
